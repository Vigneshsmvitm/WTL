The Defender not only comes with AWD (all-wheel drive) capabilities, but also features a range of off-road electronics for enhanced capabilities. Depending on the variant selected, the Defender is offered with a variety of engine options, all paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Mahindra Scorpio is an iconic full-size SUV with a dominant road presence, spacious front two rows, and a powerful 2.2-litre diesel manual engine to propel this seven or nine seater SUV. It is well suited for both city and highway driving, thanks to its airy cabin and absorbent ride.
